Hullo! This is Jingwei, a second-year PhD student from NUS Sound & Music Computing Lab supervised by Prof. Ye Wang. I'm broadly interested in computer music and machine learning. I wish my research to render a better experience in creating, enjoying, and interacting with music. My current research focuses on controllable music generation as an assistive tool for music creation and learning.

Before joining NUS, I acquired my B.E. with honors in Automation at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I was affiliated to Zhiyuan Honors Program. Since my 4th year, I have worked as a research assistant with Prof. Gus Xia at Music X Lab, NYU Shanghai.

I am a professional accordion player and an amateur Bass singer. I proudly served in SJTU Student Choir during my undergraduate study. I enjoy light music, reading, and jogging.
