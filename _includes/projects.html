Erhu Playing Techniques Detection

Project
Github Page

    • The detection of musical instrument playing techniques plays an important role in music transcription in addition to the recognition and extraction of music notions such as pitches and chords.

    • In this project, we propose a general end-to-end method based on Sound Event Detection by FCN(Fully Convolutional Networks) through a deep learning method for our musical instrument playing technique detection task. The highest accuracy of our 3 experiments on the new test set is 87.31%.
